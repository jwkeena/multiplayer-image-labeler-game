Image Labeler Game

Rules
Your goal is to describe the image using the exact same words that your online partner has also submitted. Each exactly matching word or phrase earns a point; each incorrect guess loses a life. Five lives are given per round. At the end of ten rounds, the total score is tallied. Try to get a high score!
