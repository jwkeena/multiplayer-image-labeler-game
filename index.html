Image Labeler Game

Instructions
  • Choose player 1 or player 2.
  • Set your name.
  • Wait for another player to join.
  • Start playing!

Rules
  • Your goal is to describe the random gif using the exact same words that your online partner has also submitted.
  • Each exactly matching word or phrase wins a point; each incorrect guess loses a life.
  • Three lives are given per round.
  • At the end of ten rounds, the total score is tallied.
